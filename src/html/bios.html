Harvey Dale and the Cements: About the Members

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale is the lead singer of the punkrock band. Dale grew up in Austin and has played thr guitar since he was 3 years old. His talent was clear from the start and he's been parts of different bands since he was only 10 years old.

Cement #1

Cement #1 grew up in Dallas, Texas. He first picked up drumsticks when he was only 4 years old and fell in love. He met Dale at a show in 2007. This meeting began their journey as members of the punkrock band.

Cement #2

Cement #2 grew up in Houston, Texas and has played the piano since he was sevenn years old. After being bored with classical music, Cement #2 became interested in punkrock when he was a teenager.
